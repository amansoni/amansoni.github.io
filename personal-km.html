content.digitalcyb.org

Content access interface for browsing the internet that includes a search interface. Types of content are searches, search results, media and pages. While browsing you can high-light text, or portions of screen, and make notes against these highlights. The highlights can be tagged for type and category. All content is cached and a full trail is stored. There is the ability to analyse the cache and match it to outputs as a means to consolidate the cache. Links and other media are extracted from the text and displayed in a panel.
